Why Doha, Not From a Distance

Search conducted within the market, not across it.

Much executive search reaching Qatar is run from a regional desk — the country covered between flights, its candidates drawn from a database assembled elsewhere. The result is a shortlist that could have been produced for any Gulf market, and often was.

We work differently. Each mandate is researched first-hand, in and around Doha, against the specific reality of Qatari ownership, governance and ambition. We approach leaders in confidence, assess them in person, and present only those we can stand behind — not the contents of a list.

It is the difference between coverage and counsel — and, for an appointment of consequence, the difference is the whole of it.

The Retained Standard

Why our executive search is retained.

In Qatar, search is most often sold on success — a fee paid only on a hire. That model rewards speed and volume. A retained mandate aligns us to one outcome only: the right appointment.

  • Exclusive and partner-led, from the first conversation to the appointment
  • Funded depth — original research, not a circulated shortlist
  • Each leader assessed with discipline, never extrapolation
  • Absolute confidentiality, because the mandate is ours alone
  • Engaged through onboarding, so the appointment holds
